## 10-Question Spiritual Assessment

The survey determines each user's Biblical persona through questions covering:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Relationship with the Bible">
    Current engagement level and familiarity
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Preferred Reading Times">
    When users are most likely to read
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Spiritual Content Preferences">
    Types of passages that resonate most
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reading Challenges">
    Common obstacles and pain points
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Engagement">
    Preferences for social and group features
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Motivations and Goals">
    Personal spiritual objectives
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

- Personalized strengths
- Tailored recommendations
