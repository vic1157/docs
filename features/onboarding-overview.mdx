## Onboarding Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Spiritual Assessment">
    User completes 10-question survey to determine their Biblical persona
  </Step>
  <Step title="Persona Reveal">
    Display assigned persona with strengths and recommendations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profile Creation">
    Upload photo and add personal testimony (2-step modal)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reading Goals">
    Set reading frequency and enable notifications (2-step modal)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll Walkthrough">
    Learn about Scrolls through interactive 8-step tour
  </Step>
  <Step title="Demo Scroll">
    Hands-on experience with fully interactive scroll
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Progress Tracking

The home page features an OnboardingProgress component that tracks completion:

1. ✓ Complete Your Profile
2. ✓ Set Your Reading Goals
3. ✓ RYB Scroll Walkthrough
4. Complete Your First Reading
5. Ask Leo Your First Question
6. Take Your First Quiz

<Tip>
  All onboarding states are persisted in localStorage with automatic progress updates
</Tip>
