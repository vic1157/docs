## What are Denarii?

<Accordion title="Denarii Explanation" icon="coins">
  Denarii is RYB's branded premium currency that unlocks AI-powered features while keeping core Bible reading functionality completely free forever.
</Accordion>

### Why Denarii?

- **Biblical Connection**: Named after the Roman currency mentioned in Scripture
- **Clear Value**: Users understand what they're purchasing
- **Flexible Usage**: Spend on the AI features that matter most to you
- **No Hidden Costs**: Transparent pricing with no surprise charges

## Paywall Page (`/paywall`)

The paywall is shown after:
- Survey results (persona reveal)
- Attempting to use AI features without Denarii
- Clicking "Upgrade" in app navigation

**Page Sections:**
1. **Hero**: Premium value proposition
2. **Pricing Cards**: Monthly and Annual options
3. **What are Denarii?**: Comprehensive explanation section
4. **Feature Comparison**: Free vs Premium table
5. **Trial Information**: 7-day trial details
6. **Free Plan CTA**: Continue without paying
