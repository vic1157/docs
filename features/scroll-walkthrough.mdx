## Interactive Feature Tour

Full-screen walkthrough introducing users to Scrolls with progress indicators and interactive demo elements.

## 8-Step Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome & Introduction">
    Overview of what Scrolls are and their purpose
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scroll Overview">
    Reading session metadata: passage, time estimate, verse count
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI-Generated Summary">
    - Expandable AI summary with demo text
    - Chapter context and themes
    - Show More/Less button demonstration
  </Step>

  <Step title="Questions Feature">
    - Understanding & Discussion question categories
    - Example questions with type definitions
    - HelpCircle popover explaining question types
  </Step>

  <Step title="Capture Questions">
    - Mock interactive Activity Panel
    - Demonstrates "Add Question" flow
    - Shows how to engage with Scripture during reading
  </Step>

  <Step title="Personal Reflection">
    - Journaling textarea
    - Visibility settings (Private, Friends Only, Public)
    - Entire scroll privacy controls
  </Step>

  <Step title="Starting a Scroll">
    - Explains metrics and tracking
    - Entry points (during Bible reading)
    - Active vs Inactive reading modes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete & Ready">
    - Recap of Scroll features
    - Two CTA options:
      - "Try Demo Scroll" → `/demo-scroll`
      - "Back to Home" → `/home`
  </Step>
</Steps>

- Marks completion in localStorage (`scrollWalkthroughComplete`)
- Dispatches `onboardingComplete` event
- User chooses next step (Demo Scroll or Home)
