## Scrolls

<Accordion title="What is a Scroll?" icon="scroll">
  A "Scroll" is an object that tracks active Bible reading sessions. Think of it as a reading activity record.
</Accordion>

### Scroll Contains

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Reading Range">
    Start and end points (intended reading range)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Activity Tracking">
    Questions asked during reading
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Session Duration">
    Total reading time tracked
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reflections">
    User notes after completion
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Scroll Lifecycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Scroll">
    User clicks "Start Scroll" in ActivityPanel during Bible reading
  </Step>
  <Step title="Active Reading Mode">
    Transitions from Inactive to Active mode with timer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture Activity">
    Questions, reflections, and reading time are recorded
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Scroll">
    User finalizes scroll with visibility settings (Private/Friends/Public)
  </Step>
</Steps>
